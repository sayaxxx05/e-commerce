Privacy Policy

At E-Shop, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Below, we outline the types of data we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, shipping address, and payment details to process orders, communicate with you, and provide a personalized shopping experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process your orders and deliver products
  • To improve our website and customer service
  • To send you promotional offers and updates
  • To ensure the security and functionality of our site

3. How We Protect Your Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement a variety of security measures to protect it. This includes using encryption technology and secure payment gateways.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners for processing payments and shipping.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your shopping experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences and keep track of items in your cart.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us through our support page.

7. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we do, we will post the changes on this page with an updated date.
